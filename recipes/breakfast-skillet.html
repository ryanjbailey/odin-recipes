Sunday Breakfast Skillet

Description: A staple in the Bailey household. Crispy potatoes and eggs shine, and the bacon can be substitued with a breakfast meat of your choice.

Ingredients:

  • Yukon gold potatoes
  • Large eggs (local farm, if possible)
  • Thick sliced bacon
  • Bell peppers
  • White onion

Steps:

  1. Pre-heat skillet on medium heat.
  2. Render bacon.
  3. Sweat onion and bell pepper for 3 minutes.
  4. Stir, add eggs, reduce heat, and cover for 5 minutes.
  5. Uncover when egg whites are set and yolks still runny, serve immediately.
