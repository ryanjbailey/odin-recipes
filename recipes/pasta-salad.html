Italian Pasta Salad

Description: A simple midwestern summer picnic staple. Customize it to your liking. In an ideal world you'll make your own vinaigrette, but store-bought works in a pinch, too.

Ingredients:

  • Tri-color rotini
  • Italian salami
  • Homemade italian vinaigrette
  • Whole milk provolone,
  • Fresh onion, julienned
  • Bell pepper, for crunch and color
  • Pickled vegetables, I like olives and artichoke
  • Fresh grated parmesean cheese

Steps:

  1. Cook rotini according to boxed directions
  2. Rinse cooked pasta in *COLD* water
  3. Slice/chop cheese and vegetables
  4. Toss pasta and veg with dressing
  5. Finish with fresh grated parmesean and black pepper to taste
