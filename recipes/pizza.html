Cast Iron Pizza

Description: Make your favorite pizza at home in your cast-iron skillet. Spicy sausage, chili flakes and honey give sausage pizza a whole new look. While the pizza is in the oven, throw together a salad -- simple mixed greens with balsamic or an acidic, bright fennel salad with olive and arugula.

Ingredients:

  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzerlla cheese
  • Sausage (uncased)
  • Pizza dough
  • Fresh basil

Steps:

  1. Pre-heat oven to 500F
  2. Stretch dough
  3. Spread sauce
  4. Sprinkle cheese & sausage
  5. Cook pizza, and finish with fresh basil
